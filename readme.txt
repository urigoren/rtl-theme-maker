== Installation ==

1. Upload `rtl-theme-maker.zip` onto your local computer.
2. Go to your WordPress Dashboard and select <strong>Plugins >> Add New</strong>.
3. Click on the <strong>Upload</strong> option at the top and select the `rtl-theme-maker.zip file` you just downloaded.
4. Click on <strong>Install</strong>.
5. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
6. There should be an additional RTL Theme Maker sub-menu under the Settings option of your Dashboard.
7. Clicking on the RTL Theme Maker sub-menu will cause rtl-theme-maker-page.php to be executed.
